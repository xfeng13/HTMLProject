ASU CSE180

Instructions of Accessing E-Text & MyITLab Bundle

Step #1) Go to https://my.asu.edu, enter your ASURITE ID (for example mine is xfeng13) and password to login. Next under “My Classes”, pick the right semester and click on “Computer Literacy” (hyperlinked) to go to the course Canvas website.

Step #2) Once inside the Canvas shell, from the left-hand side control panel, click on “ASU Bookstore”, then click on the title (or "Redshelf") “MyLab IT for Office 2021: Skills for Success Series + Technology in Action, 18e” to generate a unique access code, copy and paste the access code. During this procedure, you might need to turn off your pop-up blocker in order to see the new tab shown on your web browser. Click here for instructions on how to turn off your pop-up blocker. Note: you only need to do this step once. Later only use the "Access Pearson" tab to access your eText + MyLab IT bundle.

Step #3) Next, inside the same Canvas shell, from the left-hand side control panel, click on “Pearson Access”, go through the Pearson registration and login procedure, enter the access code your copied from above step #2 during the procedure.

Important: during above Pearson's registration procedure, make sure you used exactly the same name as officially listed on Canvas course website, i.e. make sure both first and last name matches what listed on Canvas class roster, for example, if your name is Bill Smith, don't register as William Smith.

Step #4) In Step #3, after clicking on Access Pearson, a new window will open. From there, you can view and complete your assignments, as well as read the eTextbook, by using the menu on the left-hand side (see below).
